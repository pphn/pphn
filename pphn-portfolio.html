Origin

Life of
Little

Born near Myanmar's Irrawaddy River, raised in Yangon. Received a first camera as a gift in 2017 and decided to become a photographer. Selected for the CDPP21 program, received the Artist Relief Fund in 2022, and exhibited at Bagan. Traveled 40+ towns, climbed 23 mountains above 3,000 feet.

"He just wants to explore the limit of human being. Just because he never trusts himself really. How far can he go?"
